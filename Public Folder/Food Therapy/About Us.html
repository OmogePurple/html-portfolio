How To Prepare Gbegiri



Gbegiri is a delicious yoruba food in Nigeria made with beans. In history, Gbegiri is said to be the local food of the peope of Oyo.
As a typical yoruba man/woman, you must love gbegiri.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • pepper
  • Palm Oil
  • Maggi
  • Salt

How to Make Gbegiri

  1. Pill the beans with water untill it is white and the skin removed
  2. put the pilled beans on a boiling water
  3. allow it to cook untill it is soft
  4. Blend the beans until it is smooth
  5. add salt pepper and maggi
